# Timeline Reference

## Historical Timeline (1922-1952)

### 1922
- **~1922:** Jens Raben born in Odense, Denmark

### 1941
- **1941:** Jens (age 19) enlists in SS, joins 16th SS Panzergrenadier Division Reichsführer-SS
- Motivated by anti-Communist ideology

### 1941-1944
- **Eastern Front:** Jens witnesses and participates in atrocities
- Becomes emotionally numb, loses ideological conviction
- Hospitalized twice (field hospitals in Kiel and Soltau)

### 1945

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|

### 1945-1949
- **Prison Camp:** Jens serves 4 years for SS membership

### 1949
- Released from prison camp
- Returns to Odense briefly
- Sister Lillian has married, lives in parents' house
- Cannot tell anyone about treasure
- Moves to Germany, assumes identity "Dieter Schmidt"
- Works with business partner Udo Dressler

### 1952

| Month | Event |
